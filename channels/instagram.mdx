Connect your Instagram Business account to Chatwoot to handle Instagram Direct Messages (DMs) from your unified inbox.

## Prerequisites

- Instagram Business or Creator account
- Facebook Page connected to your Instagram account
- Admin access to both Facebook Page and Instagram account

## Setup

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect Instagram to Facebook Page">
    First, connect your Instagram account to a Facebook Page:
    
    1. Go to Instagram Settings → Business
    2. Connect to a Facebook Page
    3. Ensure it's a Business or Creator account
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Instagram inbox in Chatwoot">
    Navigate to **Settings** → **Inboxes** → **Add Inbox** → **Instagram**
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize Facebook">
    Click **Sign in with Facebook** and grant permissions:
    - `instagram_basic` - Basic Instagram profile info
    - `instagram_manage_messages` - Send and receive messages
    - `pages_messaging` - Access via Facebook Page
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select Instagram account">
    Choose which Instagram Business account to connect from the list.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Configure and test">
    - Add agents to the inbox
    - Send a test DM to your Instagram account
    - Verify it appears in Chatwoot
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Features

- **Direct messages** - Receive and reply to Instagram DMs
- **Story mentions** - Get notified of story mentions
- **Story replies** - Respond to story replies
- **Rich media** - Send images, videos, and voice notes
- **Quick replies** - Use Instagram quick reply buttons
- **Ice breakers** - Set up conversation starters

## Message Types

### Direct Messages
Standard one-on-one conversations with Instagram users.

### Story Replies
When users reply to your Instagram Stories, conversations appear in Chatwoot.

### Story Mentions
Get notified when users mention your account in their stories.

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Cannot connect Instagram account">
    - Verify it's a Business or Creator account
    - Ensure Instagram is connected to a Facebook Page
    - Check Facebook Page admin permissions
    - Try disconnecting and reconnecting from Instagram settings
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Messages not syncing">
    - Reconnect Instagram account in Chatwoot
    - Check Facebook permissions
    - Verify Instagram account is not restricted
    - Ensure Instagram app is updated
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Cannot send messages">
    - Check if user has messaged you first (Instagram requires this)
    - Verify Instagram account status
    - Check for temporary restrictions
    - Ensure message content complies with Instagram policies
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Limitations

- Instagram requires users to message you first before you can initiate conversation
- 24-hour messaging window similar to Facebook Messenger
- Some Instagram features (polls, quizzes) don't sync to Chatwoot
- Media files have size and format limitations
